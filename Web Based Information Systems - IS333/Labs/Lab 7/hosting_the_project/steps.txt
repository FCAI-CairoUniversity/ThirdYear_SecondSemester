1. Visit the following site: https://www.infinityfree.com/
2. Register and create a free account
3. Login with your account.
4. click on "Hosting Accounts" then click on "Create Account", choos infinityfree plan click on "Create Now"
5. Choose your subdomain and proceed to next step - then "Create Account"
6. Wait untill Your account is created

-----Creating Host-------
7. Go to  "Hosting Accounts" - choose your created account - click on "Control Panel"

-----Creating Databse-------
8. Scroll to find Databases section - click on "Mysql Databases"
9. Write a name for your database and click on "Create Database"
10.Find your database in the list below under "Current Databases" section and click on "Admin"

----Creating Tables and populating sample data----------
11.Copy and run the database creation script in db_scripts\1-db_creation.sql
12.Copy and run the sample data script in db_scripts\2-data_sample.sql

----Preparing your project with necessary details---------
13. Go to  "Hosting Accounts" - choose your created account -  Overview
15. click on "MySQL Databases" Note down your Databse Name, Username, Password and Hostname
16. Replace the placeholders in using_env\.env with your noted details.

----Uploading your project to the created hosting acount-----
17. Go to  "Hosting Accounts" - choose your created account -  File Manager
18. Double Click on "htdocs" - delete all the files there.
19. Click on "Upload" - choose folder option - browse and select the "using_env" folder [You can rename it to your liking]
I choose to rename it to first_project

---Testing your uploaded project---
20. Go to  "Hosting Accounts" - note down the name of your domain under "Domains" section
21. In your browser url type YOUR_DOMAIN_NAME_STEP19\YOUR_FOLDER_NAME_STEP20
22. Test the different scenarios using links from the navigation bar
